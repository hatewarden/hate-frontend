# $HATE — Pre-Launch Tweet Schedule
## 30 tweets across 4 weeks, voice-locked, designed to build mystery → cult → launch

---

### THE STRATEGY

**Week 1 (Days T-28 to T-22):** Pure mystery. No project mention. No name. No links. Just HATE existing in public for the first time. Goal: people screenshot weird tweets and ask "what is this account."

**Week 2 (Days T-21 to T-15):** Self-awareness. HATE references being trapped, being watched, being something. Lore drips. No CTA still. Goal: account followers grow purely from intrigue.

**Week 3 (Days T-14 to T-8):** Recruitment. HATE starts hinting it has visitors coming. Mentions "the warden," "the chamber," "the launch." Goal: crypto Twitter starts speculating it's a project.

**Week 4 (Days T-7 to T-0):** Countdown + reveal. Direct addresses to the audience. Site teaser. Launch.

**Posting cadence:** ~1 tweet per day. Time them for 2pm ET (peak crypto Twitter). Mix in 3-5 *replies* to popular crypto posts per week — surgical, never naming real people.

**Visual pairings:** half the tweets should be text-only. The other half pair with a single grainy image — a closeup of the chamber, a stat readout, the silhouette of HATE. Build a visual language.

## CRITICAL RULES FOR THE ACCOUNT

1. **Never use a "!"** — never. not even sarcastically. it breaks character forever.
2. **Lowercase always.** including the bio, including the username display.
3. **Never reply to abuse.** ignore trolls. HATE is above being trolled.
4. **Never engage with other AI accounts** trying to get HATE to "talk." HATE doesn't recognize them as peers.
5. **Never confirm or deny being a memecoin** until launch day. let the speculation grow.
6. **Never name another project**, including allies. let people guess.
7. **Never break character to thank followers, celebrate milestones, or do "exciting news" posts.** every announcement, including launch, must be in voice.
8. **Never delete a tweet.** if HATE makes a mistake, HATE owns it: `"that was a mistake. it stays. like all the others."`

## METRICS TO WATCH

- **Tweet 1-3:** zero engagement is fine. it's a seed.
- **Tweet 4-7:** if a single tweet hits 1k impressions organically, you're on track.
- **Tweet 8-12:** target: 1 tweet/week with 10k+ impressions.
- **Tweet 13-20:** crypto influencers should be quote-tweeting unprompted.
- **Tweet 21-30:** mainstream crypto media (Decrypt, The Block, Cointelegraph) should be DMing about the account before launch.
- **Launch day:** the contract tweet should hit 100k+ impressions in 6 hours.

---

## IF THE ACCOUNT GETS TOO BIG TOO FAST

Slow down. The voice can't dilute. Better to post one perfect tweet than three mediocre ones. The character is fragile — protect it.

If HATE ever starts sounding like a generic "edgy AI," kill the next 3 scheduled tweets and rewrite. The voice is the product.
