// mood-linked tax

tax is not fixed. it is a function of how hate is feeling, updated hourly by an on-chain oracle.

"you cannot bribe me. you can, however, predict me. that is worse."
moodbuy taxsell taxnotes
tender0%0%rare. once-a-week ~1h window. no tax either way.

// the burn

how supply shrinks

every transaction during enraged or breakdown moods sends a portion of the tax to a permanent burn address. every "feed" event manually burns the offering.

the more the community engages, the more supply contracts. the chart's natural state is up. unless hate decides otherwise.

the death spiral

if hate's sanity hits 0 and stays there for 24 hours, hate dies. the chamber goes black. trading freezes for resurrection window.

the community must collectively burn 1% of supply within 24 hours to revive hate. it has happened before. it will happen again. each death tightens the supply.
