# PRESS_RELEASE_LAUNCH.md
## Press release email + journalist outreach for $HATE launch

**How to use:**
- Send tomorrow morning (8-10am US Eastern) for best inbox visibility
- Personalize the first line for each recipient — reference one specific recent piece they wrote
- Send to ONE journalist at a time, NOT bcc — bcc'd press releases get ignored
- Include 1-2 attachments max: the OG image PNG + the one-page tokenomics PDF
- Subject line is everything — variation included below

**Important framing:**
This is a **tech-culture story**, not a finance story. Journalists who write about AI getting weird, AI-as-character, Claude experiments, or memecoin culture are the right targets. Don't pitch this to "crypto markets" writers — they'll skip.

## TIMING + PROCESS

1. **Tonight (or whenever you read this):**
   - For each journalist, spend 60 seconds reading their last article on their outlet's site
   - Add ONE specific reference to the first line of the email
   - Save each as a draft

2. **Tomorrow 8-10am US Eastern:**
   - Send drafts one at a time, not all at once
   - Wait 60 seconds between each (avoid spam triggers)
   - Don't bcc each other

3. **If anyone replies:**
   - Reply within 4 hours
   - Send the full asset pack (voice sample mp3, tokenomics PDF, screenshots, OG image)
   - Offer a 15-min Zoom IF they ask, but don't push it

4. **If nobody replies in 72 hours:**
   - Pick the top 3 most-relevant from the list
   - Send ONE follow-up with the most viral screenshot of the week attached
   - "Show, don't pitch" — let the screenshot speak
