## OUTREACH MECHANICS — how to maximize reach with limited tools

### The three-channel reality

X DMs are NOT the primary outreach channel for crypto KOLs in 2026. Many close DMs to verified-only or mutuals-only. Here's the actual order:

**1. Telegram (best — try first)**
- Check every X bio for `t.me/handle` link
- Crypto Telegram is where deals actually close
- Estimate: ~70% of memecoin KOL deals happen via TG, not X DM

**2. Business email (slower but professional)**
- Use for serious deal proposals to YouTubers w/ business inboxes
- Subject line that works: `partnership inquiry — $HATE (Solana, Day 1)`
- Keep body under 150 words. Three lines: who you are, the deal structure, link to live site.

**3. X DM (after Premium upgrade — $3/mo)**
- Premium Basic unlocks DMs to verified-only accounts
- Don't upgrade until you have at least 10 outreach targets queued — burns first month otherwise
- Read receipts are visible; don't be discouraged by reads-without-reply (means they saw it, deal might still happen later)

**4. Public reply on pinned tweet (for closed-DM accounts)**
- This works better than people think
- Format: drop a witty reply IN HATE's voice on a recent KOL tweet. Example: replying to a chart post → "the chart agrees with you. HATE-9000 does not. hate.fund."
- ~30% of closed-DM KOLs will reply or DM YOU after a good public hook

**5. TikTok DM (almost always open)**
- TikTokers respond faster than YouTubers
- Use TikTok DM as primary contact; TikTokers don't read X DMs

**6. IG DM (50/50 — aggregator pages are bots-heavy)**
- Worth trying for aggregator pages (CryptoExplorer, ShareCrypto) where it's the primary channel
- Don't bother for personal-brand accounts (Kiyosaki, Pompliano) — won't see it

### Pre-DM warm-up (skip and you're spam)

Before sending ANY DM, do this for the recipient:
1. Follow their main account from `@hate9000`
2. Like 2-3 of their recent posts (genuine ones, not bait)
3. Reply once publicly with something in-voice
4. THEN send DM 24-48h later

Skipping warm-up halves your response rate. Estimated cost of warm-up: 5 min per KOL.

### The DM batch math

- **Goal**: 2-4 closed deals from 40 outreaches
- **Realistic response rate**: 10-25% (4-10 replies)
- **Conversion to deal**: 25-50% of replies (1-5 deals)
- **Time investment**: ~5 min warm-up + 10 min DM + 30 min follow-up = ~45 min per KOL
- **Total time for 40 outreaches**: ~30 hours over 2 weeks

Don't try to do all 40 in one sitting. Burn rate of 5-8 per day across all channels is sustainable.

### What NOT to do

- ❌ Mass-DM the same message to all 40 — they talk to each other and notice
- ❌ Lead with the deal structure in the FIRST DM (save for response, lead with hook)
- ❌ Send DM during US/EU off-hours if you want a same-day reply (best window: 9am-12pm local in their timezone)
- ❌ Follow up more than twice with no response (you're crossing into spam territory)
- ❌ Pay anyone before they post — vested via Streamflow only (see `INFLUENCER_DEAL_TEMPLATES.md`)
