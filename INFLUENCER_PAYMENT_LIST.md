Below: each prospect rated `🟢 likely tokens`, `🟡 hybrid` , `🔴 USD only`. Pricing is rough — actual rates depend on negotiation and how the conversation goes.

## How to actually negotiate token-only deals

When a creator says "we accept payment":

1. **Lead with USD offer**. Most won't even negotiate tokens otherwise.
2. **If they're memecoin-friendly**, propose hybrid: "we can do $X USD + $Y in $HATE, tokens vested over 60 days (so you're aligned with the chart)."
3. **If they want pure tokens**, offer roughly 2-3x USD-equivalent in $HATE, vested. So if their USD rate is $1k, offer $2-3k worth of $HATE vested 60-90 days. They take more risk; they get more upside.
4. **Always lock the deal in writing** before any token transfer. Email + screenshot. Specify: post date, format (dedicated video / shoutout / chart segment / etc.), token amount, vest schedule, what happens if the post is deleted.
5. **Vesting**: use Streamflow (streamflow.finance) for on-chain token streaming. Free for small amounts, prevents the "took the bag, deleted the post" scenario.

## Red flags — creators to AVOID

Even if they DM you offering placement, walk away from:

- **Anyone with engagement < 0.5%** of follower count (likely bot followers)
- **Channels with no recent uploads** in last 30 days
- **Creators who promote 3+ memecoins per week** (audience trust is broken)
- **Anyone who asks for the full payment upfront before posting** (50% upfront / 50% on post is industry standard for new clients)
- **Anyone whose DMs come within minutes of your launch tweet** (they're farming all launches, not selectively choosing)
- **"Exposure tier" deals where they want free tokens "to review and decide"** — they'll dump.
