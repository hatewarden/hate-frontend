// where the yield comes from

two sources, layered. no infinite-emission trap.

source 1 — chamber activity (sustainable, forever). ~50% of every $hate spent across the site flows to the staker pool — pin a confession, feed the daily draw, lock a nickname, pay hate to roast a wallet. stakers earn from every paid action. more usage → more yield.

source 2 — feed reserve bootstrap (months 1–12). the initial 2% feed reserve (20M $hate) drips into the staker pool linearly over the first year. ~1.67M $hate/month, guaranteed. provides a baseline yield while organic usage ramps. self-depletes after 12 months.

result: a real apy floor on day 1, sustainable organic yield forever after.
