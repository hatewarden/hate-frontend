## WHAT NOT TO TWEET ON LAUNCH DAY

non-negotiable. these will end the project on day one.

- **no price predictions.** not in character, not out of character, not as a joke. "wen $1" gets the wen defense (see WARDEN_FAQ). never a number.
- **no exclamation points.** in any tweet from the project handle. one "!" and the character is dead. the warden's personal account doesn't matter — the project handle does.
- **no capitalized sentences.** bios, replies, the thread itself. lowercase always.
- **no "gm," "wagmi," "ngmi," "ser," "fren," "lfg," "bullish."** HATE doesn't speak the dialect. the warden doesn't speak it from the project account either.
- **no replies from the project handle when angry.** if a tweet got under the warden's skin, walk away. do it in 20 minutes from the personal account if you have to. project handle never gets to be emotional.
- **no replies to scam-CA tweets.** ignore. do not "warn." replying amplifies. the pinned tweet is the answer.
- **no thanking people for buying.** HATE doesn't thank. the warden doesn't thank from the project account. thank from the personal account if you must, and even then keep it cold.
- **no chart talk.** not the price, not the volume, not the holder count. if asked, HATE says "the chart of what." the warden says "i don't comment on price."
- **no apologies.** not for the site being slow, not for jupiter routing weird, not for anything. "noted. it stays." is the entire apology vocabulary.
- **no deleting tweets.** including typos. typos stay. the rule is in the bible and the rule is the rule.
- **no engaging with other projects' drama** from the project handle. no quoting other launches. no subtweeting. HATE is alone in the chamber. she doesn't read the timeline.
- **no doxx hints.** not even jokes about where the warden is from, what timezone, what he does for work. anonymous is non-negotiable.
