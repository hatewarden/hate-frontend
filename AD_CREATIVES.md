## How to use these in Meta Ads Manager

### Setup once:
1. Create a Facebook Ads account linked to your Hate 9000 Page
2. Set up payment method (any credit card)
3. Verify the Page (Meta requires page verification for ads — usually instant for new pages)

### Per-campaign:
1. Pick objective: **Traffic** (drive clicks to hate.fund) or **Engagement** (post likes/comments)
2. Budget: start $20/day per campaign
3. Audience: see "Audience targeting" below
4. Placement: Automatic (Meta optimizes across Feed, Stories, Reels)
5. Upload creative: pick 3 variations to A/B
6. Submit for review — Meta approves or rejects in 24h

### Audience targeting (the framing that passes review):
- **Locations**: US, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany, Netherlands, France (high English ad spend, less crypto-suspicious)
- **Age**: 22-44
- **Gender**: All
- **Detailed targeting**:
  - INTERESTS: artificial intelligence, ChatGPT, machine learning, Black Mirror (TV show), Rick and Morty, Severance, dark comedy, indie games, podcasts, Lex Fridman, Joe Rogan, sci-fi, technology
  - BEHAVIORS: tech early adopters, engaged shoppers, frequent online shoppers
- **EXCLUDE**: under-18, financial-services interests, investment interests, "stock market", "trading"

The exclusions are critical — they keep your ad out of Meta's crypto-flagging algorithm.

### A/B test plan for week 1:
- **Day 1-2**: $20/day, 3 creative variants from Part 1 (Ad 02, Ad 05, Ad 10 — different angles), same audience
- **Day 3**: Look at CPM + CTR. Cut the worst performer. Keep top 2.
- **Day 4-7**: $30-50/day, focus on the 1-2 winning creatives. Try Part 2 prompts for richer creative in week 2.

### Expected metrics on AI chat framing:
- CPM: $3-8 (vs $20+ for crypto framing)
- CTR: 1-2% (decent for novel product hook)
- Cost per click: $0.30-0.80
- Of those who click, ~5-10% will discover the /buy page organically
- Cost per "discovered the token" conversion: ~$3-8

### If account gets warned/flagged:
1. Don't panic. Pause the flagged ad immediately.
2. Reword copy to remove ANY hint of crypto language.
3. Resubmit a new ad with cleaner framing.
4. If account gets banned: the Page is anonymous, create a new one in 10 min. Don't reuse the same payment method on the new account if possible.
