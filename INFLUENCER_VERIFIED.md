# INFLUENCER_VERIFIED.md
## verified currently-active crypto creators (as of 2026-05-14)

**Verification date**: 2026-05-14
**Verification method**: WebSearch across multiple 2026 influencer-ranking sources (FeedSpot, Modash, Heepsy, CoinLaunch, Coinbound, AJ Marketing, NFT Plazas, CryptoNinjas, BeInCrypto, Cointelegraph, TradersUnion) cross-referenced against direct profile lookups and 2026 news coverage. Where multiple sources gave different follower counts, the most recent / most cited number was used.

**Context for HATE outreach**: A late-April 2026 YouTube purge wiped out roughly 35M combined crypto subscribers (Bitcoin.com, Bitcoin Magazine, and others terminated for "harmful and dangerous" content). This has compressed viewership across all surviving crypto YouTubers — Carl Moon publicly stated his views are now below 2018 bear-market levels (~15-20K per video). This is GOOD news for our pitch: surviving YouTubers are hungrier for paid deals, and vested-token deals look more attractive when ad revenue is suppressed. It also means our risk model should weight "still posting in last 7 days" extra heavily.

**Legend**:
- 🟢 = high likelihood of accepting token-only or hybrid deal (smaller channel, hungry, or known to take memecoin deals)
- 🟡 = will probably want hybrid (some cash + tokens), but vested deals plausible
- 🔴 = cash-only / major brand, vested-token deal unlikely without big cash component
